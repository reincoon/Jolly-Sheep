Introduction

What is the essential story being told by your site and what type of structure did you choose to implement.

This site is built for people who's hobby is knitting, crochet and toy making. Here people can find inspiration from works of others, some tutorials and patterns, and even friends during visiting the events and commenting others' pictures of handicrafts. I have implemented a page briefly telling about other four pages, second page consists of free patterns, third page is announcing the events and fairs, fourth page is a Gallery of a pictures of handicrafts made by users, and fifth page consists of articles and tutorials for all levels. Users are different age from the very young teenagers to senior users of all genders. The site is responsible for three screens: desktop, tablet and mobile.
