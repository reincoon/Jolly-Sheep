Crochet Fair

Join us at our Crochet Fair in Notting Hill, London where those who mastered the art of crocheting will display their most beautiful pieces. Who will be crowned crocheting champion?

Knitting Festival in 09/2022

Fancy a knitted top for autumn? Then look no further than to this year’s Knitting Festival in Chelsea, London, starting at 11am on 5 September 2022, giving you the chance to choose from tons of different knits. This event is free to attend.
